3. The activation number statistics table is displayed according to the agent with the activation rate. When swiping left and right on the activation rate agent, the activation number will switch to display the agent synchronously;

地区统计：

1、统计代理、国家、地区时间纬度内切割的次数；

2、统计纬度：近7天、30天、90天；


3、国家地区：根据筛选显示具体地区下的时间纬度内的总切割次数；

4、国家地区过多时，页面报表可左右滑动查看更多；


Regional statistics:

1. Count the number of cuts within the time dimension of the agent, country, and region;

2. Statistical latitude: the past 7 days, 30 days, and 90 days;

3. Country and region: Display the total number of cuts within the time dimension of the specific region based on the filter;

4. When there are too many countries and regions, the page report can be swiped left and right to view more;


数据总览：

1、总览统计登录代理商及其链条下代理商名下设备总数、激活总数、总授权次数、已使用次数、客户总数、直属下级数量；


分类摘要：

1、摘要数据统计登录代理商及其链条下代理商的数据；

2、查询条件：代理商筛选，只可选择登录账号及其链条下所有代理商账号，国家、地区筛选默认显示所有国家、地区，级联选择。均为单选。选择国家后，地区显示该国家下的地区。

3、选择代理商后，只显示代理商及其链条下有关联的国家地区选择；选择国家地区后，只显示该国家地区的代理商；


4、分类摘要下所有报表受到筛选条件，代理商、国家、地区的控制，更改此三个条件同步影响下列所有报表，时间纬度默认回近7天；

5、时间纬度每个报表可单独选择；


Data Overview:

1. The summary statistics include the total number of devices, activations, authorizations, usages, customers, and direct subordinates of the logged-in agent and the agents in its chain;


Classification Summary:

1. The summary statistics include the data of the logged-in agent and the agents in its chain;

2. Query conditions: For agent filtering, only the logged-in account and all agent accounts in its chain can be selected. For country and region filtering, all countries and regions are displayed by default, and cascade selection is performed. All are single selections. After selecting a country, the region displays the region under the country.

3. After selecting an agent, only the country and region selections related to the agent and its chain are displayed; after selecting a country and region, only the agents in the country and region are displayed;

4. All reports under the classification summary are subject to the filtering conditions, agents, countries, and regions. Changing these three conditions will affect all the following reports simultaneously. The time dimension defaults to the past 7 days;

5. Each report in the time dimension can be selected separately;
